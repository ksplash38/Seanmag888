Seanmag 888 Offers Discounts to registered non-profit organizations and registered charities

Seanmag 888 provide a discount to registered non-profit organizations and registered charities. In both cases, we require the following information to determine your eligibility:

  1. Registered name of non-profit organization or charity
  2. Registered non-profit organization or charity number
    • For customers in Australia: ABN or CAN
    • For customers in in the continent of Africa: Business number/Registration for country registered.
    • For customers in UK: CRN
    • For customers in Canada:nine-digitBN/Registration number
    • For customers in U.S.:EIN number

 

As the discount can only be applied to the registered non-profit organization or charity, approved orders must be placed under our respective sales email and the company name listed as such.

Please get in touch with our sales team at sales@saenmag888.com, where you will receive a fast response.

What we will do after your organization is approved for a discount.

Once your organization is approved for a discount, we will email you a quote for the item(s) you are ordering, and this will include the discount.

Please note that this offer is only valid towards regular priced products and cannot be used with another promotion, discount, or promo code. It will not be applicable to sale items.
